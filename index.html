Professional activities

I spend my research time in the "Plant Fiber" from the UMR UGSF 8476 (CNRS and University of Lille). The main research topic of the team is using 'Omics' tools to investigate plant cell physiology and metabolism, with a particular focus on plant cell wall.

I am also a teacher at the University of Lille in charge of the courses in cell biology, biology and plant physiology. I give theoretical courses, but also directed and practical works. Convinced that our teachings must be oriented towards excellence and international, I took the direction of the studies of the Master International Plant Sciences. As a geek, I naturally turned to the use of new information technologies to develop innovative projects to help students succeed in their studies.
